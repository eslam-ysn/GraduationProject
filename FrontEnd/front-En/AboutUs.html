About Our Project

We are a student group from The Hashemite University dedicated to developing innovative solutions that enhance customer experience and decision-making. Our project is an Evaluation System (Customer Sentiment Analysis) designed to collect, analyze, and visualize customer feedback efficiently.

The system consists of two main parts:

  • Customer Rating Interface: Allows customers to provide feedback quickly and accurately, with built-in mechanisms to prevent incorrect or invalid ratings.
  • Administrative Dashboard: Enables authorized personnel to view, manage, and analyze feedback data through an intuitive interface.

A key highlight of our project is the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) modules in both Arabic and English, capable of classifying customer evaluations as Positive, Negative, or Neutral. This feature helps organizations gain deeper insights into customer sentiment and make data-driven improvements to their services. Our goal is to provide a smart, multilingual, and reliable feedback system that bridges the gap between customers and decision-makers.
